To add a module:

1. Create the folder structure:
   Modules/<ModuleName>/Sources/
   Modules/<ModuleName>/Tests/

2. Add the target in Project.swift:
   moduleTarget(name: "<ModuleName>")

3. Add it to the app target's dependencies:
   .target(name: "<ModuleName>")

4. Run:
   tuist generate --no-open
